## Resume Tips

### Quantify Impact
- ‚ùå "Improved test coverage"
- ‚úÖ "Increased test coverage from 45% to 85%, reducing production bugs by 30%"

### Use Action Verbs
- Implemented, Designed, Led, Reduced, Improved, Increased, Automated

### Tailor to Job Description
Match keywords from job posting:
- Job says "API testing" ‚Üí Highlight API testing experience
- Job says "CI/CD" ‚Üí Mention your CI/CD work
- Job says "Agile" ‚Üí Include Agile/Scrum experience

### Keep It Concise
- 1 page for <5 years experience
- 2 pages for 5+ years experience
- No more than 6-8 bullets per job
