# Hardware QA

Quality validation for physical products through engineering validation (EVT), design validation (DVT), and production validation (PVT) phases.

## Lessons

1. [EVT, DVT, PVT Explained](01-evt-dvt-pvt-explained.md)
2. [Manufacturing Quality Lifecycle](02-manufacturing-quality-lifecycle.md)
3. [Environmental Testing](03-environmental-testing.md)
4. [Power and Battery Testing](04-power-battery-testing.md)
5. [Firmware Validation](05-firmware-validation.md)
6. [Supplier Quality Management](06-supplier-quality.md)
7. [Failure Analysis](07-failure-analysis.md)
8. [8D and CAPA Process](08-8d-capa-process.md)
9. [Measurement and Uncertainty](09-measurement-uncertainty.md)

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will:
- Understand the hardware validation lifecycle (EVT/DVT/PVT)
- Design and execute environmental test plans
- Validate power systems and battery performance
- Conduct firmware testing for embedded systems
- Manage supplier quality
- Perform root cause analysis on hardware failures
- Apply 8D methodology for corrective actions
- Understand measurement uncertainty in testing

## Practical Application

Hardware QA is essential for:
- Consumer electronics companies
- IoT device manufacturers
- Medical device companies
- Automotive suppliers
- Industrial equipment manufacturers

These concepts apply whether you're validating smartphones, wearables, smart home devices, medical equipment, or automotive components.
